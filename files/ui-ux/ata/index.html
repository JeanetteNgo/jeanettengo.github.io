Aureus Trial Adventure

Duration Feb 2023
My Role UI/UX Designer
Tools Used Figma, Figjam, LucidChart

Introduction

Aureus Trial Adventure (ATA) is a digitalisation project that seeks to transform the classroom experience for trial music lessons. Instead of using physical activity booklets, teachers can now utilize the ATA app to conduct their lessons. The target audience: young students aged 3-6 years old whose parents have signed them up for a trial music lesson with Aureus.

v1.0.0 was designed and developed over the course of mid-February to March 2023, and released in April 2023. This version is a minimum viable product featuring a basic set of layouts and flows for 6 instruments.

My Role

    As the lead designer, I:
  • Worked collaboratively with directors and developers to ideate and iterate on designs
  • Created functioning prototypes for product demos

Process

The team invited a trainer to walk us through the current classroom experience.

We discovered that in a single trial lesson, teachers have to switch between multiple mediums such as videos, printed booklets, and external websites.

Objective

The objective of this digitalisation project is to streamline the classroom trial experience, both for the teachers conducting the lesson, and the students engaging with it.


I designed the iconography for the inbox feature, with the goal to create recognizable icons that are representative of the features they serve.

Ideation

Since we are building upon an existing product, the process was a lot more simplified. I asked some questions, collaborated with other designers to come up with a storyboard, and finalised the flows and requirements.

Design & Development

Given the short timeline for development, we optimised the build process by constraining the designs to reusable layouts.

Project Gallery

Concluding Thoughts

The project concluded as a successful trial run with positive engagement and feedback from teachers, students and parents. This kickstarted plans to expand on the project to encompass not just trial classes, but also normal classes.
