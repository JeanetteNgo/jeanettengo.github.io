OneService Chatbot Design

Duration Sep 2021
Project Type Individual, Competition
Role Conceptualization, Design
Tools Used Adobe Illustrator

Overview

This is a design submission for the OneService Chatbot Personality Competition.

Design Writeup:
Hoodie (derived from the “Love Our 'Hood” Initiative), is an amiable little robot whose sunny disposition makes interacting with them a joy. Currently, Hoodie has no specific gender. Hoodie likes growing greens in their spare time, and can be easily identified from a distance with their bright yellow hood and little green sprout. Hoodie has a simple character design to make recreation of the character easy and accessible to all.
