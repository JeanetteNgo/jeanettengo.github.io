Overview

This is a project submission for my NM3216 Game Design module. In this project, I worked as the lead designer in charge of spearheading the overall game direction and revising the game mechanics and rules to offer players a fun and engaging experience.

Introduction

Corner Rush is a 4 player strategic board game suitable for ages 8 and up. In this game, the main objective is to reach the token that is placed at the edge of the board diagonally opposite to the player's own pawn.
